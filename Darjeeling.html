DARJEELING

Overview

On a summer day, the fragrance of tea leaves lifts off the hills and hover over Darjeeling like a hat and the wonderful things breathes unsaid inside that, like the bent trees, the happy earthworms, the reluctant tea-colored puddles, flowering Rhododendrons and Magnolia plants and the wild garden flowers blooming in different colors of rainbow and a British bungalow growing a fragile façade of greenery for ages amidst a sprawling tea garden. Darjeeling tour packages offer an enchanting experience amidst the mesmerizing beauty of its tourist places, leaving travelers awestruck and captivated. The northwest winds visit Darjeeling to tear it full of holes but the new beautiful things come in the first spit of spring on the northwest wind, and the old leaves go, not one lasts but the beauty stays with the new and lasts for eternity. Darjeeling is a beauty that won’t let your heart stop, from beating in ecstasy, and the tourist places in Darjeeling shall always ensure that.

Attractions

  • Darjeeling Himalayan Railway (Toy Train):- A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, also known as the Toy Train, offers a mesmerizing journey through the lush green hills and quaint villages. The train ride is undoubtedly one of the best places to visit in Darjeeling.
  • Tiger Hill :- To witness the awe-inspiring sight of the sun rising over the snow-clad peaks of Kanchenjunga, make your way to Tiger Hill, one of the top 5 places to visit in Darjeeling. The panoramic views from here will leave you spellbound.
  • Batasia Loop :- To witness the awe-inspiring sight of the sun rising over the snow-clad peaks of Kanchenjunga, make your way to Tiger Hill, one of the top 5 places to visit in Darjeeling. The panoramic views from here will leave you spellbound.
  • Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park:- Established in 1958, this zoological park is home to several rare and endangered species like the red panda, snow leopard, and Himalayan wolf. It is one of the best places to visit in Darjeeling and Gangtok for wildlife enthusiasts.

Transportation

Getting to Darjeeling:

  • By train: The railhead closest to Darjeeling is located at New Jalpaiguri. This station offers good connectivity with the rest of the country. A number of trains are available from Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai, Bengaluru and Kochi.
  • By Road: Darjeeling has good road connectivity with cities like Gangtok (96 km) and Kalimpong (50 km). Regular buses are available for Darjeeling from nearby towns like Siliguri, Gangtok, Kalimpong and Kurseong. Though the buses are an economical option, these are quite time consuming and it is recommended to take a cab instead of travelling in buses.
  • By air: So those thinking of coming to Darjeeling from far away cities can get their air tickets booked till Bagdogra airport, which is the nearest air base serving the hill town. A number of carriers including Air India, Jet Airways, SpiceJet and Indigo operate regular flights from cities like Delhi, Guwahati and Kolkata to Darjeeling.

Getting around Darjeeling:

  • Walking: All the trails have signboards marking the way and the locals are quite helpful.
  • Rickshaw: Hand-pulled rickshaws are available to hire. Walking is quicker than being pulled along in a rickshaw.
  • Visitors can choose the mode of transportation that suits their budget and preferences to reach Darjeeling and explore the town.
