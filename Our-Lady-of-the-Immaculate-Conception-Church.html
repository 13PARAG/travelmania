OUR LADY OF THE IMMACULATE CONCEPTION CHURCH

Overview

The Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception Church in Panjim, Goa, India is a captivating blend of architectural styles that harmoniously combine to create its distinctive personality.The church is located in Panjim and sits atop a hill facing the square below. The city's municipal garden (Garcia da orta) lies to its southeast and can be seen from atop the hill. Some key features of the Church include: Architectural Fusion: The church's construction began in 1541 and was completed in 1609 over several centuries, incorporating various influences. The beautiful carvings on the exterior, the majestic entrance, and the gorgeous altar inside showcase this design. Exterior: The church's white facade, adorned with intricate details, signifies the Immaculate Virgin, Mary. Two flights of zigzag stairs ascend to the hillside where the church stands. The tall belfry, centered atop the facade, houses the ancient bell retrieved from the Augustinian ruins of the Church of Our Lady of Grace in Old Goa. Interior: Inside, the grandiosity is enhanced by towering ceilings and elaborate woodwork panels. The altar, serving as the room's focal point, is embellished with golden figures and decorations. Annual Celebration: On December 8, the church celebrates the annual feast of Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception. The church, lit up at night, draws a large crowd of residents and visitors during this festive occasion.

Attractions

  • The imposing facade, with two towers and a centrally placed taller belfry, is often referred to as the “crown” of Panjim.
  • Its primary architectural style is Portuguese Baroque, characterized by exquisite craftsmanship, curved lines, and lavish ornamentation
  • A large fair, brilliant lights, blue and white flowers (symbolizing complementary color schemes), musical band performances, and fireworks follow the prayers and processions

Transportation

Getting to Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception Church:

  • By train: The nearest railway station to the Basilica of Bom Jesus is Karmali Railway Station, which is situated about 10 kilometers from Panaji.
  • By bus: From Panaji, the state capital, you can: Take a taxi or hire a taxi to go directly to the church. Catch a bus from the Panaji bus stand and get off near the church, then walk for a few kilometers until you arrive. The church is approximately 10 kilometers away from Panaji, and the journey takes about 30 minutes
  • By air: The nearest airport to Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception Church is Goa (GOI) Airport, which is approximately 12.6 kilometers (7.8 miles) away

Getting around Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception Church:

  • Walking: All the trails have signboards marking the way and the locals are quite helpful.
  • Rickshaw: Hand-pulled rickshaws are available to hire. Walking is quicker than being pulled along in a rickshaw.
  • Visitors can choose the mode of transportation that suits their budget and preferences to reach the church and explore the town.
